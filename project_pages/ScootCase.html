ScootCase

This was the final project for the graduate-level course, Design for Manufacturability. The goal was to create a fictitious product by combining two existing items and perform many aspects of product development for it. We combined a suitcase and a scooter to create the ScootCase. The development of the ScootCase included:

    VOC Worksheets and Interpreted Customer Needs
    Product Architecture Schematic
    QFD 1+2
    Product Specification
    BOM with Preliminary Cost Estimate
    Detailed Cost Estimate
    Target Costing Analysis
    CAD (Design for Assembly)
    Fishbone Assembly Diagram
    roduct Development Timeline
    Cost-Worth Analysis
    Boothroyd-Dewhurst Assembly Analysis
